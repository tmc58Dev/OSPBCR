ABOUT THE REGISTRY

Odisha State Population-Based Cancer Registry

A statewide public health initiative dedicated to systematic cancer surveillance, epidemiological research, and evidence-based cancer control planning across Odisha.

Registry Overview

The Odisha State Population-Based Cancer Registry (OS-PBCR) has been established to generate reliable and representative cancer incidence information across the state.


The registry supports public health planning, resource allocation, cancer prevention initiatives, and research activities through standardized collection and analysis of cancer data from healthcare facilities and reporting centers.

Core Purpose

  • Population-Based Cancer Surveillance
  • Monitoring Cancer Trends
  • Research Facilitation
  • Policy Development Support
  • Healthcare Planning
